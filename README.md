## About

I am a Brazilian researcher, consultant, and professor with a background in <strong>Finance, Applied Statistics, and Quantitative Methods</strong>. My early work was grounded in <strong>Econometrics</strong>; in recent years, I have expanded that foundation into <strong>Psychometrics, Machine Learning, and Reproducible Workflows</strong>.
